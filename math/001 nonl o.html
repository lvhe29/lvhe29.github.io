A IMPORTANT THEOREM

$f \in C^2(\Omega)$
$\Omega \subseteq R$ is an open convex set
$\Rightarrow$
① $\forall x \in \Omega, \nabla^2 f \succeq 0$ $\Leftrightarrow$ $f$ is convex.
② $\forall x \in \Omega, \nabla^2 f \succ 0$ $\Rightarrow$ $f$ is strictly convex.

Question:
Is founction $f: R^n \rightarrow R \ $ convex?

Solution:

  1. Compute the gradient $\nabla f$ of $f$ .
  2. Compute the hessian matrix $\nabla^2 f$ of $f$ .
  3. Check if the hessian is positive semidefinite.
